# Photographer Attributions — Pallas Cat LoRA v1

The v1 training dataset (92 images) draws on photographs from 
wildlife photographers, zoo livestream archives, and Creative 
Commons sources on iNaturalist and Flickr.

If you recognize your work here and would like a correction, 
removal, or different credit format, please open an issue.
